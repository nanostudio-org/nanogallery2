nanogallery2 creates beautiful and high quality photo galleries





nanogallery2 is focused on performance and on providing the best experience to user with any device. Galleries are highly customizable: choose between justified, grid or cascading layout and style it to suit your personnal needs.


Content source

nanogallery2 uses the photos from where you already store them: Flickr, Google Photos or your web server. You don't have to change anything on your current photo management. Just point nanogallery2 to your existing content on the web. Naturally, you can also self host your photos on your personal website. Gallery customization is done regardless of your content source.

Instant ready

You don't need to read the full documentation to start: use the builder to quickly and easily generate HTML templates and publish them on your website in a couple of minutes.

Eye-catching effects

Hundreds of transitions and effects to capture the attention of your visitors. Use presets or create complex effects very easily.

Seamless integration in your workflow

With nanogallery2, you can publish your photos to your website without any break in your standard way to handle your photos.
Mobile phone -> Google Photos -> your website with nanogallery2
DSRL -> Adobe Lightroom -> Google Photos -> your website with nanogallery2


What's new since nanoGALLERY?

nanogallery2 is following the popular nanoGALLERY script. The display algorithm has been completly rewritten for a better user experience. New features include: filter by keywords, shopping cart, social sharing, advanced hover effects, styling, tools on thumbnails, more button, thumbnail display transitions, navigation in albums without breadcrumb, ...

Features

Image gallery with lightbox, multiple responsive layouts, thumbnail display animation and hover effects, mobile friendly, smart lazy load, deep linking, browser back/forward, Flickr, Google Photos, self hosted images, display location/GPS with Google Maps, gesture (swipe and pinch zoom), album and sub-albums, breadcrumb, filter on tags, social sharing, tools on thumbnail, etc.

Browser support

Firefox 6+ - Chrome 16+ - Edge 13+ - IE 10+ - Opera 12.1+ - Yandex 14.12+
